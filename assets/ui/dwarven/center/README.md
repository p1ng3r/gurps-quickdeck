# UI2 Center Modular Art Assets

These assets skin the GURPS QuickDeck UI2 center panel.

Rules:
- Modular pieces only. Do not replace the center panel with one giant image.
- No baked text.
- No layout assumptions.
- Assets must tolerate center-only, left-open, right-open, and both-open states.
- Prefer stretch-safe plates, repeatable textures, and edge-safe decoration.
- Transparent WebP where useful.
- Keep contrast readable behind text/buttons.

Expected files:
- center-iron-tile.webp
  - Repeatable tile for center cockpit background.
  - Suggested: 320x320 or 512x512.

- actor-card-iron-plate.webp
  - Stretch-safe actor card background.
  - Suggested: 768x256 or 1024x320.

- quick-slots-panel-iron.webp
  - Stretch-safe Action Deck panel background.
  - Suggested: 768x320.

- quick-slot-row-iron-rivets.webp
  - Stretch-safe info row/card plate.
  - Suggested: 512x96.

- dropdown-toggle-iron.webp
  - Stretch-safe Combat/Skills/Spells toggle strip.
  - Suggested: 512x64.

- button-small-bronze.webp
  - Stretch-safe small rectangular button.
  - Suggested: 96x48 or 128x64.

- button-round-bronze.webp
  - Round/square button art for roll/carousel buttons.
  - Suggested: 96x96.

- header-strip-iron-brass.webp
  - Horizontal repeating/stretched header chrome strip.
  - Suggested: 768x48 or 1024x64.
